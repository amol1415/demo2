JACKS

ESTD BARS 1963

If you fancy stopping by, propping up the bar and having a drink in Waterloo, then Jacks is the place to be!
If you’re looking for a spot for after-work beers in Southwark then be sure to drop in. Located near the Southbank, we have an
extensive wine list and an authentic Thai menu to match. Our offer extends beyond the bar with takeaway and delivery on our food menu.

OUR STORY

Jack’s Bar and Kitchen is the brainchild of Ryan and John McElhinney. We are located at 96 Isabella Street (behind Southwark
Tube Station) SE1 8DD. The original Jack’s was opened in Ireland in 1970 by their father Jack, so the boys decided to pay
tribute to their father by re-opening the original almost forty years on. Jack's is a bar for birthdays, catching up with friends,
lunch on our patio, craft beer, cocktails and delicious Thai food.

We cater for all events so enquire now by emailing: jacks@windmilltaverns.com

MENUS

Our authentic Thai food "Johnny Rice" is served Monday - Friday for lunch.

Grab your takeaway boxes, order your drinks, and find a table in our outdoor area to enjoy your afternoon.
